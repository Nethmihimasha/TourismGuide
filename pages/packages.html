5-Day Cultural Journey

1

Arrival in Colombo

Airport transfer, city tour, check-in hotel. Welcome dinner at a rooftop restaurant.

2

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Early morning climb of the UNESCO-listed rock fortress. Afternoon visit to Dambulla Cave Temple.

3

Polonnaruwa Ancient City

Explore the medieval capital on bicycle, visiting ancient stupas and royal palaces.

4

Kandy & Temple of Tooth

Drive to Kandy via spice garden. Evening cultural dance show and temple visit.

5

Hill Country Train

Iconic train journey to Ella through tea plantations. Visit Nine Arch Bridge at sunset.
