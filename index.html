Why Sri Lanka?

A World of Wonders in
One Small Island

Sri Lanka is one of Asia's most diverse travel destinations. Within a few hours, visitors can experience misty mountains, tropical beaches, wildlife safaris, ancient cities, waterfalls, tea plantations, and vibrant cultural festivals.

Whether you seek adventure, relaxation, or history — Sri Lanka offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler, wrapped in warm local hospitality.

Essentials

Travel Tips for Sri Lanka

Best Time to Visit

Dec–Mar for southwest coast; May–Sep for east coast

Visa

ETA visa required. Apply online before arrival

Currency

Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). ATMs widely available

Weather

Tropical climate, 27–32°C year-round

Language

Sinhala & Tamil. English widely spoken

Transport

Tuk-tuks, trains & buses. Private drivers recommended
